What changed, in plain language

Each pair below shows one thing we took away (red) and the simpler idea we put in its place (green).

Removed Glass morphism

The blurry, frosted-glass look behind cards.

Replaced backdrop-filter: blur() and semi-transparent fills with solid surface colors.

Added Clean surfaces

Plain, solid backgrounds that keep text easy to read.

Dark mode: #1a2736. Light mode: #ffffff. Thin 1px borders, minimal shadow.

Removed Neon glow

The buzzing cyan/green halos around cards and text.

Large box-shadow glows, glowing text, and pulsing animations are gone.

Added Subtle elevation

Cards still lift on hover — just gently.

A small shadow for depth. 1px lift on hover. No color bleed or glow.

Removed Decorative overlays

Scanlines, grid patterns, corner brackets, and noise textures.

None of these survived the cleanup. Every surface is now plain.

Added Clear typography

Normal sentence case, standard fonts, readable sizes.

11–13px labels, weight 500–600, no extreme letter-spacing or ALL CAPS everywhere.

Removed Cyberpunk fonts

The sci-fi Orbitron headings were everywhere.

Replaced with system-friendly Inter. JetBrains Mono is kept only for numbers/values.

Added Colored icon chips

Small rounded squares with tinted backgrounds.

One color per category (blue, green, amber, purple) — no borders, no glow shadows.

Removed Ambient orbs

Floating blurs behind the app and in card corners.

Including animated gradient underlines on the top header.

Added Quiet hover states

Hovering a row or nav item just changes the background — nothing flashy.

Uses a subtle surfaceHover token instead of colored tint.

Removed Heavy animations

Plasma glows, matrix rain, glitch, and aurora pulses.

plasmaGlow, neonGlow, auroraPulse, glitch, hologram, rgbShift, matrixRain — all retired.

Added Minimal scrollbars

Thin, gray scrollbars that stay out of the way.

5px width, transparent track, subtle thumb. Firefox-friendly via scrollbar-width: thin.
